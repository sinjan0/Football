Bayern Munich

Bayern Munich was founded in 1900. The club was baised in the city of Munich. Bayern Munich was formed when members of the MTV 1879 sports club broke away to form their own club. After World War 2, football in West Germany was divided into 5 diffrent leagues, the top divions were called Oberliga. Bayern Munich played played in the Oberliga South. In 1963, the bundisliga was made, and Bayern Munich was promoted to it in the 1965-1966 season. Bayern Munich eventually started to gain trophies. In 1974-1976, they won 3 European cups in a row. The club's success in the 60s and 70s propelled it to the front of german football. In total, the football club has won 28 Bundisliga titles, the DFB-Pokal 19 times, and the league cup 6 times.
