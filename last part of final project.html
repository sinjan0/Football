My Favorite Football Player

Harry Kane

Harry Kane was born in the July of 1993. Harry Kane had one older brother. He played football from a young age and he eventually joined his local club, Rigdeway Rovers. After joining his first club he then joined the Arsenal youth academy, but he was released from it for not being "athletic". He eventually got a growthe spurt that made him taller and stronger. On his 16th birthday, he signed a scholarship contract with tottenham. In the 2009-2010 season, he scored 18 times for tottenham's under 18 team. He made his first team apperance on 25th of August, 2011, scoring 5 goals in 18 matches. from 2010-2014 he had multiple loan spells from Millwall to Norwich city. In the 2014-2015 seasonhe won the PFA Young Player of the Year, scoring 21 goals. From 2015-2016 he scored the most goals in the Premier League. His squad number also changed that season from number 18 to number 10. After a 748-minute drought he scored his first goal of the season against Manchester City. That season, he scored 25 league goals and he won the golden boot. In the 2016-2017 season he was the runner up for the epl and he won his second golden boot. On the 1st December of 2016, Kane signed a new contract with Tottenham. On the 20th of April 2017, named in the PFA Team of the Year award for the 3rd consectutive season. By the end of the season he had scored 29 league goals. In 2018, Harry Kane made it to the Champions League final and to the world cup semi final. In the 2022-2023 season, he scored 30 league goals, broke tottenham's all time scoring record, but in the same season he left the club to go to Bayern Munich to win some trophies.
