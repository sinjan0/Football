History of Football

Creation of Football

During the 1100s of football, a sport was created. It was the beginning of football, but back then it too violent so the english goverment banned it. It wasn't until 19th century that the sport unbanned. In the 1800s the game was far diffrnt then it is now, back then in some schools you could carry the ball in your hand but in other schools you couldn't. The diffrent rules made it hard for other teams to play against each other. It was in 1863 that finally the first football association was made to normalize the rules of football.

Early Football

The industrial revolution changed football. Not only did children play the game, but adults also started to play the game. Eventually the first football clubs began to form, Thus Sheffield Fc became the first football club in 1857. As football began to gain attraction, the EPL or the (English Football League) was made.

International attraction

For a lot of it's history football was a British sport, slowy then the sport spread around the world. This and the fact that other contries started to make thier own football associations led to the creation of the Federation Internationale de Football Association or better known as FIFA. FIFA provided the rules for international matches. Until the 1930s the most important international football competition was the Olympic Football Tournament, but then the FIFA world cup was made.

Football Currently

Football is now one the most watched sports in the world. Although the game has changed many times the history and the tradition of the game still runs fresh though out Football.
